Mars Rover!

Move the Rover with

  • Up, Down, Left and Right Arrows to move forward, backward and turning the rover!
  • Opening the browser Console and passing:
    • Rover[0].moveForward()
    • Rover[0].moveBackward()
    • Rover[0].turnLeft()
    • Rover[0].turnRight()
    • Rover[0].autoPilot(str)
      • Use a string of 'f','b','r' and 'l'

  • Or pass a string command here and the click run!

  • Anytime you want you can refresh the page to generate a new terrain!
  • Avoid hiting the rocks! Have fun!
